# SCG Readymix Indonesia 🏗️

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **SCG Readymix Indonesia**. We are committed to delivering high-quality concrete solutions and driving digital transformation in the construction industry.

## 🚀 About Us
SCG Readymix Indonesia is a leading provider of ready-mixed concrete. This organization serves as a hub for our internal tools, digital integration projects, and collaborative development to enhance our operational efficiency and customer experience.

### 🛠️ What We Build
We focus on developing data-driven solutions and automated systems to optimize our operational excellence:

- **Stocktake Monitoring Online** 📊
  Real-time digital inventory management system to ensure data accuracy between physical stock and system records across all plants.
  
- **ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) Pipelines** ⚙️
  Robust data integration processes that collect, clean, and centralize information from various production sources into our data warehouse for advanced analytics.
  
- **Web Scraping & Data Mining** 🕸️
  Automated data collection tools to gather market insights, material pricing, and competitive intelligence to support strategic decision-making.
